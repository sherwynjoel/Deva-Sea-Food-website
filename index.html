About Deva Sea Food

Rooted in Coastal Heritage, Driven by Global Standards

Deva Sea Food stands as a distinguished Indian exporter of premium pasteurized crab meat and frozen seafood, proudly serving leading importers, distributors, and foodservice brands across Thailand, the United States, Europe, the United Kingdom, and the UAE.

Inspired by the rich coastal legacy of southern India, we thoughtfully unite time-honoured harvesting traditions with state-of-the-art processing technologies. Every step—from sourcing through trusted coastal communities to precision-controlled cooking, packing, and cold-chain logistics—is designed to safeguard purity, texture, and natural flavour.

Our disciplined approach ensures that every consignment entrusted to our global clients reflects refinement, reliability, and uncompromising international standards. With unwavering integrity, we have cultivated enduring relationships with partners who value authenticity, consistency, and long-term collaboration.
