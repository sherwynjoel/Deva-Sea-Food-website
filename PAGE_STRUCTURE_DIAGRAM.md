## Section Summary

1. **Header** (Sticky) - Logo, brand name, navigation menu
2. **Brand Banner** - Full-width promotional image
3. **Hero** - Main headline, CTA buttons, hero image
4. **About** - Company introduction with highlight card
5. **Vision & Mission** - Two-column cards
6. **Why Choose Us** - 6 feature cards in grid
7. **Products** - 3 product cards with detailed lists
8. **Quality & Sustainability** - Content + 4 quality items grid
9. **Contact** - Contact info + inquiry form
10. **Footer** - Brand name, tagline, full address

## Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Dark blue gradients (#031328, #2543d8)
- **Accent**: Light blue (#73e0ff, #7fd7ff)
- **Background**: Light blue (#f5f9ff) for alt sections
- **Text**: White on dark, dark blue on light

## Responsive Breakpoints
- **Desktop**: Full layout (1120px max-width)
- **Tablet** (≤960px): 2-column grids become single column
- **Mobile** (≤768px): Navigation becomes hamburger menu, single column layouts
- **Small Mobile** (≤480px): Adjusted spacing and font sizes
